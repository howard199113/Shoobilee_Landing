How it works

We make it easy for our members to do good

1 - QUEUE

QUEUE favorite new shoes, receive them in the mail.

2 - WEAR

WEAR for as long and as many times as desired.

3 - KEEP or Return

KEEP or RETURN, you decide. We regift all returns to partners.

4 - REPEAT

REPEAT the steps and enjoy unlimited pairs every month!

Every return is donated.

Shoobilee donates every returned pair to our local charity partners that support women in need. Together with you, we bridge the two worlds by turning mourning hearts into dancing feet.
